Global Talent Management Framework

This site is intended primarily to support Talent Management leaders across the globe. Our aim is for Network alignment in how we approach and run Talent Management. The content is intended to enable 'freedom within a framework' - providing good-practice tools, templates and guidelines, but room for customization to meet your local needs.
